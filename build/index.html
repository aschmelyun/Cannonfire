Wrappers

Fluid

This demo page is an example of a fluid wrapper. It's size changes relative to the width of the browser window.

  • 1200px and up: 90% wide
  • Between 768px and 1199px: 96% wide
  • Below 768px: 100% wide

Fixed

Fixed wrappers have particular widths that they remain at depending on the width of the browser window.

  • 1200px and up: 1160px wide
  • Between 992px and 1199px: 960px wide
  • Between 768px and 991px: 740px wide
  • Below 768px: 100% wide
